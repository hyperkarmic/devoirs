All About Me

I’m from Stockport, and reside in North Manchester a mile from the city, because I love urban spaces, and the suburbs bore me!

I liquidated my life savings to pay for ‘coding boot-camp’, quit my job and spent six months teaching myself the rudiments of computer science basics. I’ve always loved computers. I love crisp, clear web-design. All this is a big risk, and a massive life-change for me, but if you do what you’ve always done, you’ll get what you’ve always got, right???

In my previous existence, I was a social care professional. I specialized in supporting neuro-diverse young people, and providing positive role modelling, although I have worked in many different residential, community and institutional settings over the past two decades. I hope to pass coding skills onto young people when I have finished my current training – although I want the core of my professional activity and energy to centre around emerging the technologies and their effective and aesthetic deployment!

My other interests include vegetarian cooking (especially Indian food which I just adore)! I love punk rock, alternative music and old school hip-hop. I love European/Eastern philosophy, sociology, cinema and literature. I can read French, and I study Russian in my spare time….and I spend too much time playing around with Linux set ups on old beat-up legacy laptops! Obscure anime and Soviet era animation holds a great fascination for me also.

I can’t wait to start my career in tech – and hope to make a unique, passionate and effective addition to the teams I find myself embedded with!!!
